The Last of the Leaves

In the small town of Autumn Hollow, November had arrived with the quiet grace of a fading dream. The air was crisp, carrying the smell of wood smoke and the remnants of fallen leaves. The trees, once vibrant with oranges and reds, now stood bare against a sky that threatened rain but never quite delivered

An old oak tree at the edge of the town park was the last to give up its leaves. Each morning, Emma would walk past it, the soft crunch of dried leaves beneath her boots marking the passing days. She had been coming to this park for as long as she could remember v.
